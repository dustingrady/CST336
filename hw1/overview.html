Computer Viruses

Overview Worms Viruses Trojans


Computer Viruses are carefully crafted pieces of software

which (much like biological viruses) are intended to infect

and spread from one host to the next.


This website addresses a few of the different types of computer

viruses out there, and the common misconceptions some people have

about them.
