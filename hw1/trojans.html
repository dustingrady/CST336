Trojans

Overview Worms Viruses Trojans


Unlike viruses, trojans are non-replicating code. They can be the payload that a worm allows

to be executed, or they can simply be disguised as a legitimate piece of software. Trojans, once

activated, can perform various actions on the infected system, such as deleting, blocking, modifying

or copying data as well as disrupting the performance of a computer or computer network.
