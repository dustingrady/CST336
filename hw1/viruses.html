Viruses

Overview Worms Viruses Trojans


The term "computer virus" is common these days and is used as an umbrella term

for worms, trojans, cryptoviruses, etc. A virus is defined as a type of software

that replicates itself by modifying other programs and inserting it's own code.

Unlike worms or trojans, a virus is self replicating and carries a payload, making

them very powerful, especially when paired with a zero day exploit.
